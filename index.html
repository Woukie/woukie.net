WoukieNet | Blue Oasis 2025

A well known fact within the animal kingdom is that creatures possessing broad vision are known as 'prey,' while those with narrow vision are known as 'predators.'

It is a troubling fact that a minority of programmers have evolved to possess an expansive field of vision, to the extent where they require costly curved monitors that stretch around their heads to accommodate the constraints of their flawed biology.

WoukieNet is a paradise for programmers who do not consider themselves to be part of this subset. By entering this site, you acknowledge and embrace 4:3 as the divinely ordained aspect ratio.
